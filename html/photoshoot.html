Photoshoot Day - The Process

For photography class, I was assigned to take portraits that focused on lighting. I spent hours experimenting with different angles and settings to capture the perfect shot with my little sister Shelby. My sister was a great sport, patiently posing for each shot as I adjusted the camera settings. With her silly energy, I was able to create some truly magical photos that captured her joyful spirit.

Shelby is a part of the dance team at her high school. Using manual mode, I was able to capture some shots of her in mid-air.

She also found a manakin head prop and immediately started posing with it. I got some very interesting photos with her posing with the manakin head.
